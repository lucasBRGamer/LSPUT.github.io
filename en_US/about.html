Why choose L.S.P. UT?

L.S.P. UT was based on the idea of diversify your games, making them more fun and unique and is the only free way without bans. With ModSkin, you can use any League skin without spending a cent. We have an active support at Discord and we are always available to help you. And if you've liked a lot our project, you can make us a donation.

Know more

Modern and interactive design

ModSkin brings impressive details that gives users access to gold borders, rarity indicator, and a chest icon, that shows if you can get a skin through loot or not.

Easy, and updated

ModSkin is always updated with league, and always bring diverse tematics of the game to itself. It is simple and intuitive, with decorative border in the champions, making ModSkin beatiful to your eyes. On the upper corner you can see a bar where it has your recently used champions, and in one click you cant access and change to other skins.
